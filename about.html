About Matthew

Matthew Chamberlain is a web developer, poet, artist, and musician living in Wellington New Zealand. He has developed a mimalist asthetic for his web sites and is looking forward to branching out and trying new things in the workforce. In particular he has an intrest in learning more about back end coding and also java script. He would also like to learn Ruby and Python.

He is interested in philosophy, politics and economics in addition to web development and the arts. His musical influences include, Swans, Grimes, Boards of Canada, Kyary Pamyu Pamyu, Perfume, Godspeed you! Black Emperor, John Cage, spectral composers, Philip Glass, Rachmananov, Mahler and Paganini. He is also influenced by various black metal, ambient electronic, industrial, post punk, Jpop, Kpop and gothic musicians. His favourite artists are H. R. Gieger, Francis Bacon and Salvador Dali.
